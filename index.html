COLO COFFEE: KONG
Popup

Pop-up shop design combining Syracuse University’s School of Architecture and Colo Coffee from Colombia. Inspired by the idea of a temple, the space blends daily rituals with moments of meditation.
CHILLHOUSE
Ringo Studio Intern Project

Retail design for Chillhouse, a modern beauty brand centered on self-care and lifestyle. The space merges a nail salon and café into one cohesive environment.
Public Intimacy: Runway
Performative Spatial Study

Research on the evolution of runway shows as performative spaces shaped by changing relationships between models and audiences. The study culminates in a peep-show model that reinterprets the act of viewing and informs future approaches to retail design.
X-RAY
Scenography

Installation design based on the concept of “reveal,” exploring how hidden elements can be perceived through shadow. The work invites viewers to experience space indirectly, uncovering what is usually unseen.
WATER WAVE
Scenography

Installation inspired by the historical and environmental context of the Erie Canal. The design reinterprets the rhythmic movement of water to evoke memory and create a nocturnal experience of light, motion, and atmosphere.
DESTINATION
Scenography

Site-specific installation along the Erie Canal in Syracuse, visualizing the time and distance of cargo movement between locks. The projection transforms data into a dynamic display that connects historical infrastructure with contemporary experience.
EXTENDING THE BODY
Experimental Garment Installation

Design experiment exploring how material behavior can define garment structure. Using balloons and stockings, the study investigates elasticity, gravity, and tension to generate natural draping formed by weight.
URBAN PARCHMENT
Plaza

Urban design proposal reimagining the Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall Plaza as a space where memory and everyday life coexist. The project introduces new programs and circulation to soften its monumental character and reconnect the site with contemporary urban life.
NEWLY ICON
Museum

Expansion proposal for the Taipei Fine Arts Museum addressing the lack of collection space in the existing building. The project introduces a new east wing and explores the spatial dialogue between old and new museums, reinterpreting how exhibitions may evolve in the future.
HIVE LIVING
Housing

Housing design exploring future modes of living and community formation within the urban environment. The project introduces the concept of porosity to challenge standardized high-rise typologies and respond to shifting family structures and short-term residency patterns.
LAYER BY LAYER
Hospitality

Architectural design that mediates between old and new through form and structure. The project introduces a connective path through the site, fostering encounters and linking the building with its surroundings and the larger urban context.
